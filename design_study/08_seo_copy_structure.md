# SEO Copy Structure & Metadata Guide

This document outlines the standardized structure for SEO metadata (Title Tags and Meta Descriptions) across the Boostify USA website. These guidelines ensure consistency, maximize click-through rates (CTR) in search engine results pages (SERPs), and properly target local search intent.

## 1. Title Tag Architecture

The overarching formula for Boostify USA title tags is the **"Keyword + Benefit"** structure. 

### The Formula
`[Primary Keyword / Page Topic] : [Benefit-Driven Action or Value Proposition]`
*(A pipe `|` can occasionally be used instead of a colon if the right side is a list of services rather than a sentence fragment).*

### Core Rules
1. **No Dates or Bracketed Tags**: Do NOT use `(2026)` or `[Updated]` in titles. Titles should remain evergreen, clean, and professional.
2. **Front-Load Keywords**: The most important search term (e.g., "Local SEO", "Visalia Marketing Agency") must appear at the very beginning of the title.
3. **Sell the Click**: The second half of the title (after the colon) must provide a compelling reason to click—focusing on outcomes, quality, or action.
4. **Length**: Keep titles under 60 characters whenever possible to avoid truncation in Google search results.

### Examples of the Formula in Action
* **Service Pages:**
  * `Web Design: High-Converting Custom Local Websites`
  * `Local SEO: Rank Higher & Get Found by Customers`
  * `Local Marketing: Generate Consistent High-Quality Leads`
* **City Marketing Pages:**
  * `Fresno Marketing Agency: Drive Local Revenue & Growth`
  * `Visalia Marketing Agency: Top-Rated Web Design & SEO`
  * `Tulare Marketing Agency: Turn Clicks Into Customers`
  * `Sanger Marketing Agency: Custom Websites That Convert`

## 2. Meta Description Guidelines

Meta descriptions do not directly impact rankings, but they heavily influence whether a user clicks your link. 

### Core Rules
1. **Local Relevance**: Always mention the specific geographic area the page targets (e.g., "Fresno", "Tulare County", "Kings County"). Do not be generic.
2. **Primary Services Mentions**: Quickly list the core services offered (e.g., "custom web design, local SEO, and Google Ads").
3. **The Outcome Hook**: End the description with the ultimate benefit to the business owner (e.g., "turn visitors into customers", "dominate Google Maps").
4. **Length**: Keep descriptions around 150-155 characters.

### Examples of Good Meta Descriptions
* *"Custom, SEO-optimized web design for Fresno businesses. Mobile-first, fast loading, and built to convert visitors into customers."*
* *"Dominate Google Maps and local search results in Fresno. Our local SEO strategies help you get found by neighbors who need your services."*
* *"Boostify USA is a leading Visalia marketing agency. Custom web design, local SEO, and Google Ads that drive real growth for Tulare County businesses."*

## 3. Implementation Checklist
Whenever a new page is created or an existing page is updated, verify:
- [ ] Title follows the `Keyword : Benefit` format.
- [ ] No `(Year)` or `[Updated]` modifiers in the title.
- [ ] Meta description mentions the specific city or county.
- [ ] Meta description clearly states the services offered.
- [ ] Both title and description use confident, results-oriented language aligned with the `07_copy_branding.md` guide.
